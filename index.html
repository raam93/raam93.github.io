Hello!

I’m a PhD student in the Faculty of Information at the University of Toronto, co-advised by Ishtiaque Ahmed and Shion Guha. I'm also a member of the Third Space research group and Dynamics Graphics Project lab in the Department of Computer Science.

I'm broadly interested in the area of fairness in sociotechnical systems and developing technologies to help these systems produce beneficial outcomes to society. In this regard, two potential fields of inquiry I'm interested in are machine learning and social networks.

Before starting my PhD, I worked as a Data Scientist at Everwell Health Solutions and as a pre-doctoral AI Center Fellow at Microsoft Research India. During these years, I have designed data hygiene frameworks, built dashboards, developed optimal incentive schmes for spreading public awareness messages, built open-source software to explain ML models, and studied caste hierarchy and information flow within online social networks of Indian politicians. Check out my publications for more information and feel free to email me to collaborate!
